Lasagna

Description

Lasagna (or lasagne) is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, alternating with fillings such as meat sauce (ragù), béchamel sauce, vegetables, and cheeses (typically ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan). The dish is assembled and then baked in an oven.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • A rich meat sauce
  • A creamy cheese filling

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F
  2. Prepare the meat sauce
  3. Make the cheese filling
  4. Cook the lasagna noodles
  5. Assemble the lasagna i
  6. Bake the lasagna
  7. Uncover and brown the top
  8. Rest the lasagna on a wire rack
