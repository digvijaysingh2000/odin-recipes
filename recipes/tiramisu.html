Tiramisu

Description

Tiramisu is a popular Italian dessert made of coffee-soaked ladyfingers and layers of whipped mascarpone cream, flavored with cocoa powder. The name comes from the Italian tirami su, meaning "pick me up" or "cheer me up", referencing the caffeine and high-calorie ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Egg Yolks
  • Sugar
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Mascarpone
  • Coffee
  • Rum
  • Ladyfingers
  • Cocoa Powder

Steps

  1. Make the Filling
  2. Make the Whipped Cream
  3. Soak the Ladyfingers
  4. Assemble the Tiramisu
