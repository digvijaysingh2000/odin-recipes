Sausage Balls

Description

Sausage balls are a classic Southern American appetizer made from a combination of ground sausage, shredded cheese, and all-purpose baking mix (such as Bisquick), baked into savory, bite-sized spheres. They are a popular, easy-to-make party food, especially during the holidays or for game-day gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork sausage, at room temperature
  • 2 cups biscuit baking mix
  • 12 ounces sharp Cheddar cheese, shredded

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine room temperature sausage, biscuit mix, and shredded Cheddar together in a large bowl; mix with your hands until well combined.
  3. Roll mixture into about 30 walnut-sized balls; transfer to a baking sheet.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and sausage is cooked through, 20 to 25 minutes.
